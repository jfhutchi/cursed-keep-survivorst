# Ethical Monetization Guardrails

Reward Loop Seed contains no monetization. These rules document what may be considered later and what stays banned.

## Allowed Later

- Cosmetic purchases with clear contents.
- Premium unlocks with clear contents.
- Remove-ads purchase if ads ever exist.
- DLC or content packs with clear contents.
- Optional rewarded-ad bonus chest as a future stub only, never required for core fun.

## Banned

- Paid loot boxes.
- Paid randomized rewards.
- Premium-currency loot boxes.
- Real-money random power.
- Pay-to-win upgrades.
- Hidden odds.
- Fake urgency.
- Streak punishment.
- Forced ads.
- Manipulative notifications.
- Child-targeted monetization.
- Dark patterns.

## Free Chest Rules

- Free chests must be earned through gameplay.
- Free chests must not require payment.
- Free chests must not require premium currency.
- Free chests must not require ads in this prototype.
- Free chests may contain run bonuses, score bonuses, cosmetic placeholders, or earned currency that cannot be bought with real money.

## Optional Rewarded Ads Later

Optional rewarded ads may be considered only if the base game remains enjoyable without them. A rewarded-ad chest must be clearly optional, must not punish players who skip it, and must not grant paid randomized power.

## Zero-Spend Standard

The game must stay fun with zero purchases. Monetization may add expression, convenience, or clearly described content, but it must not make the unpaid game worse.
